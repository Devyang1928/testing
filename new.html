    What are the Levels of German Course

    Level A1

    Helps you familiarize yourself with expressions and phrases to convey phrases related to the satisfaction of your specific needs.

    Level A2

    Can introduce themselves and ask the same questions as others, frequently using expressions related to subjects directly related to them.

    Level B1

    Can express one's opinion briefly on specific or known topics such as work, study, and leisure.

    Level B2

    Can you easily communicate with the locals? Understand the main content of complex texts, identify strengths, and highlight weaknesses.

    Level C1

    Can interpret a wide variety of difficult material and deduce hidden meanings. You can also communicate fluently without having to search for words frequently and extensively.

    Level C2

    You can write a lot and understand almost everything you read or hear. Summarize information from a variety of written and oral sources and use the language effectively in all areas.
